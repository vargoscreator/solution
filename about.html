We Make Things Differently

Occlusion Solutions company is a team of oil and gas professionals with extensive global experience, which helps us to do things differently when retiring oil and gas wells.


Our 150+ years of experience sets us apart significantly since it represents continuous growth and evolution rather than merely repeating the same year 150 times.

Pushing Boundaries
in Legacy Practices

Unlike typical decommissioning oil and gas company in the industry, we blend our owner/operator background with advanced technology. We move away from outdated methods, ensuring safe and economically feasible operations. No more outdated flip phones, welcome to the digital era!

Utilising Expertise
and Digital Technologies

With our deep industry knowledge and with the help of the latest digital solutions, we transform disruptions into long-term value. We boost productivity, optimize budgets, and streamline end-to-end processes by embracing the latest technologies.

Converting
Liabilities to Assets

We specialize in turning plugging liabilities into retired assets, capturing potential revenue streams along the way. Our strategic approach maximizes the value of decommissioned oil and gas infrastructure, providing sustainable and profitable outcomes.
